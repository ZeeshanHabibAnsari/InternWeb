About Us

Creating unique places among nature’s beauty

For more than three decades, Discovery Land Company has built the world’s finest private residential communities.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to create extraordinary places where families can connect with nature and each other, Discovery Land Company has grown into a global leader in luxury private residential club communities and resorts.

From our first community to over 40 destinations worldwide, we have remained committed to one principle: every property should reflect the unique character of its landscape, culture, and people.

Our communities are not simply developments — they are living environments where architecture, hospitality, and nature exist in perfect harmony.

Our Mission

We believe a home should be as unique as your family. Our mission is to create private residential communities that encourage families to build unforgettable memories in the world’s most beautiful settings.

Each community incorporates local architecture, culture, and cuisine to deliver an authentic and immersive experience. Generously welcoming and reassuringly exclusive, we elevate the art of living while fostering a true sense of belonging.

Discovery invites you to experience a world where adventure, comfort, and community come together — building a legacy that endures for generations.
